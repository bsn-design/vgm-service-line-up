How do I get my account all set up?

Back to Product Questions
  • Go to Account Settings
  • Fill out the Company Information section with the details you will want shown on your Service Line Up account.
  • Fill out the date/time information
    • Time Zone- Select your Time Zone
    • Send Schedules- If a User/Contractor has the Send Schedules option selected, this is the time when their schedule will be sent to them (+/- 15 minutes).
  • Fill out the Company Email section
    • Email Address- Enter in the email address that you will send email from
    • Email Password- Enter in the password for your email account.
    • SMTP Outbound Server Details- Enter in your SMTP details here.
      • NOTE- With Gmail, when you first send an email through Service Line Up, you will get an email saying Suspicious sign in prevented.
      • Click on the link below the Reset password button in the sentence that says: “If this was you, and you are having trouble accessing your account, complete the troubleshooting steps listed at http://support.google.com/mail?p=client_login”
      • Click on the link within step 3, that says: “If this was you, and you are having trouble accessing your account, complete the troubleshooting steps listed at http://support.google.com/mail?p=client_login”
      • Click on the Continue button
  • Fill out the Credit Card Processing section
    • If you signed up with our preferred merchant, you should have received an email with your ePN Account number and Restriction Key.
    • ePN Account- Enter in your ePN Account number
    • Restrict Key- Enter in your Restrict Key
  • Fill out the Billing Information section.
    • Depending on how you bill your customers will determine how you fill out this section
    • Billing Type- Choose your billing type
    • Enter in your defaults that will be displayed throughout Service Line Up
  • Enter in your other default options.
    • Advertisement Dropdown- Add/Edit/Delete items shown in this dropdown
    • Adjustment Dropdown- Add/Edit/Delete items shown in this dropdown
    • Payment Dropdown- Add/Edit/Delete items shown in this dropdown
    • Click on Save Changes

How do I create users?

Back to Product Questions

Note- your personal user is already set up. Edit/view it by selecting it from the User drop down.

  • Choose a User Type
    • No Access- Does not allow any access
    • Contractor- Only allows Schedule page, Reports (Payroll Report & Appointment by Contractor Report), for that particular Contractor only to be viewed
    • Manager- Can see and do everything except:
      • See the Users page
      • See the Analytics page
      • See the Franchise page
      • See anyone else besides themselves on the Payroll Report
      • Ability to use the Export Customers button
      • Ability to delete customers
    • Manager & Contractor- Is listed on the Schedule page and can see and do everything except:
      • See the Users page
      • See the Analytics page
      • See the Franchise page
      • See anyone else besides themselves on the Payroll Report
      • Ability to use the Export Customers button
      • Ability to delete customers
    • Company Owner- Can see and do everything except:
      • Create Company Owner Users
    • Company Owner & Contractor- Is listed on the Schedule page and can see and do everything except:
      • Create Company Owner Users
  • Enter all the needed information
    • Team Name- Team names are grouped together at the top of the Schedule page.
    • Username- Enter in a user name, can be any combination of letters/numbers.
    • Password-Enter in a password. Must be at least 6 characters.
    • Payment Day- The day you would like an individual Payroll Report sent to the User.

How do I book an existing customer?

Back to Product Questions

There are three ways:

  1. Schedule Page
    1. Go to the Schedule page.
    2. Enter in the Search box any part of the customers first/last name, or phone number.
    3. Select the customer you want, and click on Search.
    4. Select an available contractor
    5. Enter in the Date and Time of the appointment
    6. Select a recurrence
    7. Ensure all the information is correct and click on Save Changes.
  2. Customer Page Option 1
    1. Go to the Customer page.
    2. Enter in the Search box any part of the customers first/last name, or phone number.
    3. Select the customer you want, and click on Search.
    4. Ensure you have all the needed information.
    5. Click on the Schedule button
    6. On the Schedule page, select an available contractor
    7. Enter in the Date and Time of the appointment
    8. Select a recurrence
    9. Ensure all the information is correct and click on Save Changes.
  3. Customer Page Option 2
    1. Go to the Customer page.
    2. Enter in the Search box any part of the customers first/last name, or phone number.
    3. Select the customer you want, and click on Search.
    4. Ensure you have all the needed information.
    5. Click on New Appointment
    6. Enter in the Date and Time of the appointment
    7. Select a recurrence
    8. Add Contractors
    9. Ensure all the information is correct and click on Save Changes.

What are the different recurrences I can do for a customer?

Back to Product Questions
  • None- Will not recur.
  • Weekly- Used on customers with appointments that occur every X number of weeks. (i.e. bi-weekly, every 4 weeks, etc.)
  • Monthly- Used for customers that want to be monthly on a certain week and day.
  • Re-Make Future Appointments- Used to update all future appointments with the new information.

What is the purpose of each report?

Back to Product Questions
  • Appointments by Contractor Report- Is sent with the User/Contractor to show the details of their jobs for the day.

    Note- This is also the report that gets emailed to the Users/Contractors that have this option enabled.

  • Scheduling Appointment Report- Useful when doing schedules. Lists all the customers and any important information listed in the Scheduling Notes section on the Customer page.
  • Confirmation Appointment Report- Used when confirming Appointments the day before. Can Click on Verified or Left Message to know how you confirmed with them.
  • Contractor Directory- Lists all the Active contractors their email address and phone numbers
  • Payroll Report- Used for contractors payroll, and to view totals for the dates selected.
  • Unavailability Report- Shows the date and times that the contractors need to be unavailable and when they put in their unavailability.
  • New/Follow-up Customers Reports- Shows the customers you went to that you need to follow-up with that have a New or Follow-up status on the Customer page.
  • Keys Report- Shows the keys needed for the dates selected. Also allows you to mark the keys as Returned.
  • Sales Report- Shows the Sales for the dates selected.
