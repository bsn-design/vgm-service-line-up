Main Features

Security

All pages are behind a certified SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) certificate. This allows sensitive information such as credit card numbers, social security numbers, and login credentials to be transmitted securely.

Data is stored in a secure up to date Microsoft SQL 2012 database.

Sensitive database data is encrypted with a 128 bit AES key.

Passwords must be at least 8 characters long and contain numbers as well as upper and lower case characters.

Enhanced form protection against SQL injections and buffer overruns.

Accessibility

You and all your users can log into Service Line Up from anywhere that the internet is available. We have also designed this system to work on tablets and phones, so your company is only a click away wherever you are.

Reports

We have your bases covered with all the reports you will need. 9 different reports are included with Service Line Up. Appointments by Contractor, Scheduling, Confirmation, Contractor Directory, Payroll, Unavailability, New/Follow-up Customers, Keys, and Sales Reports.

Scheduling

Ideal for seeing who is available to work and booking customers. Shows total booked hours by contractor and as a whole. Many useful features make this page invaluable, such as the Compact Mode, which only shows you your availability, making it very easy to see availability.

Collecting Payments

With just a click of a button you can get paid, do returns, or send invoices. If you use our preferred merchant provider, you can also do all your credit card transactions directly through our system rather than having to input data twice into another system.

Customer Information

This is a powerful section of the software that lets you do many different functions, from storing customer information, adding appointments, updating customer notes, marking specifics needed by the customer, to specifying how often they want you to come and what time of day is best for them.

Bidding a Job

Specifically designed for when you are doing a quote over the phone. Step by step questions tailored to get all the information needed in order to give the customer a quote over the phone. Even better, all the information is transferred to the Customer page if they end up booking so you don’t have to re-enter anything.

Users / Contractors Information

Anytime you want to add/edit or view a contractor this is where you go. Keep track of how and when they get paid, important notes, and many other options designed to make your job as easy as possible. As new users are created or edited, this is where you will do it. With the ability to assign users different user roles, you are able to set limitations. The user role someone is assigned will determine whether they can view or do certain things within Service Line Up.

Account Settings Page

All your different company specific settings will be stored here. Company information, Email, Credit Card Processing, and Billing Type information is all in this area.

Return Policy

If for any reason you are not happy with our product, you can cancel your account within 30 days of your initial purchase and we will refund your money, no questions asked. After the 30 day period if you choose to cancel your account, we will not bill you again and your account will remain open for the duration of your billing cycle.
