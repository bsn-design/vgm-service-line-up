Security

Service Line Up was built with highest possible security in mind. Not only do we use 128 bit AES key SSL security throughout the site, we also use a secure up to date Microsoft SQL 2012 database with enhanced form protection against SQL injections and buffer overruns.

When you sign up with our preferred merchant you gain the ability to use our system to its highest potential. You will be able to charge your customers credit cards directly through Service Line Up . Eliminate having to duplicate data in another system. With great rates, no contracts and no setup fee it is an easy choice.

Scheduling

Easily do scheduling with our smart scheduler. See what days and times you have available and schedule customers quickly and easily. Once you schedule a customer on a recurring appointment the system will generate their appointment automatically so you know what your schedule looks like weeks in advance.

Payments

Get paid with the click of a button through Service Line Up. By signing up with our preferred merchant you gain the ability to do all your credit card processing directly through our system. Customers

Contractors

All of your contractors will be easy to keep track of with Service Line Up. Knowing when they get paid and how they get paid is all in one place.

Reports

From Payroll to Appointment Reports, Service Line Up has it all. The reports are designed to give you as much information as possible. They have been field tested so we know what information you will need to get your contractors to their job on time with all the information they will need.
